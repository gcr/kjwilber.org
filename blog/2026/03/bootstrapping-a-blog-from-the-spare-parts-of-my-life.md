How to subscribe

For now, there are two ways to subscribe to my content: bookmarks and RSS feed. If you use substack, … 
BLuesky??

But be warned: this is a scrapbooking project, not a platform. You won’t learn who I am except parasoxially, and I don’t care to appeal to what others do. This is an exercise in developing my internal appreciation and taste for things that I like, so that’s the singular person I’m writing to.

I don’t care to grow my audience, and for now I don’t really care to put more effort into this than that which ifs necessary to sustain the habit.
